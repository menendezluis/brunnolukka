BIOGRAPHY

Brunno Lukka is a DJ and producer focused on House, Tech House, and Techno for the dance floor. His sound blends Latin grooves with modern electronic production, creating tracks designed for clubs and late-night energy.

Based in Buenos Aires, Brunno is developing his project through consistent releases, DJ sets, and a growing online audience. His music is driven by groove, bass, and vocal hooks that connect quickly with listeners.

Whether playing in intimate venues or larger stages, the goal is always the same: keep the groove moving.
